Dolores, Eastern Samar

Dolores, Eastern Samar, is my province. It is a place I visit every few months. Getting there is pretty rough, literally, roads going there are full of potholes. It's a 3-6 hour drive going there from Tacloban, plus the extra 1-2 hours going to Tacloban using a plane. You'll be lucky to arrive there within 5 hours from Manila. It's worth it once you arrive though. Dolores has good beaches, alright roads, but most importantly, a ton of food. /p>

What to do in Dolores: Go to the beach, go island hopping, or go eat in a restaurant or cafe.
